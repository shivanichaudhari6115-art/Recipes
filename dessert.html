COFFEE CHOCOLATE MOUSSE

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Serving: 2

Ingredients

200 gms Dark chocolate
1 tsp Coffee powder
2-3 tbsp Butter
1 cup Whipping cream

For Garnish

Orange wedges
Dragon fruit wedges
Grapes, sliced
Mint leaves
Icing sugar

Process:

1. Slice the chocolate block using Philip's mixer with it's slicing attachment.
2. Then add the chocolate in a bowl and place on the boiling water to melt the chocolate.
3. Once chocolate is melted add coffee powder, butter in it and mix everything properly.
4. Meanwhile in a blender jar add whipping cream and blend it until soft peaks.
Then add some to the chocolate mixture in it and blend it again.
5. Now remove this mixture in a bowl and mix in the remaining chocolate mixture.
6. To serve the mousse, take a serving glass and apply some chocolate on its inner surface and fill it with the chocolate cream mixture, keep in the fridge to set for 30-35 minutes.
7. Remove and garnish with orange wedges, dragon fruit wedges, sliced grapes, mint leaves, icing sugar.
